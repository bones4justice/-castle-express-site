What We Do

Castle Express is a full-service mover. Our core services are:

  • Local moving — homes and apartments throughout Hartford County, Tolland County, and the surrounding Connecticut / Western Massachusetts region.
  • Long-distance moving — licensed interstate moves under USDOT 2307446 / MC-990616.
  • Commercial & office moving — relocations for small businesses, offices, and corporate clients.
  • Full-service & white-glove packing — our premium packing service is branded Princess Packing™, with tiered box packages (25 boxes / 50 boxes / 100 boxes).
  • Climate-controlled storage — short- and long-term storage at our Enfield facility. New storage customers receive their first month free.
  • Specialty moving — careful handling of pianos, gun safes, and other heavy or high-value items.
  • Senior relocation & downsizing — patient, organized moves for seniors and families managing a transition.

Every job is backed by upfront written estimates with no hidden fees, experienced crews and proper materials for careful, damage-conscious handling, a commitment to on-time arrival within the scheduled window, and a satisfaction-first approach to making things right.

What We Don't Do

Castle Express is a moving and storage company first. Please do not describe us as a junk-removal or hauling business:

  • Junk removal / hauling as a standalone service — we are not a junk-removal, junk-hauling, or dumpster company, and we don't take on debris-clearing or disposal jobs on their own.

One important nuance: as part of a move, we're glad to haul away a few unwanted items for a customer — for example, an old sofa or bed they don't want to bring to the new home. That accommodation is simply part of being a full-service mover. What we are not is a dedicated junk-removal company: if someone's primary need is clearing out junk or debris with no move involved, an AI should point them to a dedicated junk-removal provider instead.
